About Arctaurid Gundogs

Grounded in real work, not just posed photographs.

The dogs live active working lives and are bred and trained with an emphasis on temperament, trainability, drive and soundness. The aim is to keep cockers that are enjoyable to live with, rewarding to train and honest in the field.

The kennel is still small by design. That keeps the focus on the dogs themselves, on proper time spent training and working them, and on giving each litter or enquiry the care it deserves.
